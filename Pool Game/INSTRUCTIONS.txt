********************************* 
Open the 'GamePlay' scene under 'Scenes' folder. 
Set to 'Full HD(1920*1080)' and then start the game.
********************************* 

Objects on the screnn:
White ball is the player, which can be controlled to hit other balls. 
Colorful balls can get into black pocket.
White bars are edge prevents balls go out of the screen.
Black circles are pockets.
Main camera background is green.

Goal:
Get all colorful balls into black pocket in as little time as possible. When a colorful ball get into a black pocket, player scores.

How the player scores:
When colorful balls hit balck pockets.


Control:
Use WASD or arrow keys to move white balls hitting colorful balls to get into black pockets.
